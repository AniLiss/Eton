Technologies of the new opportunities.

Etton is an IT company, the software developer, the provider of the IT and consulting solutions.

The company specializes in providing business and industrial solutions, as well as creating the platforms working with Big Data.

One of the company’s areas covers the creation of the global technological portals to interact with big audience. In our projects, we use all the available modern technologies of providing the content and data operations.

the company in figures

  • Over 50 information systems, portal and mobile solutions.
  • More than 3 000 000 users.
  • The team comprising more than 100 developers.

our projects

The information and analytical system “The open budget for youth”

The global sports resource to popularize a healthy lifestyle and physical training. The portal helps users to choose and take up a certain kind of sport and make the personal achievements publically available.

What we did:

We developed the concept of the global portal.

The III Winter Military World Games

The portal is an interactive platform providing online all the available information about the winter military games. The content is adjusted according to the users’ requests, roles and interests, geolocation and other parameters. The project implements all the modern technologies of providing and targeting the content.

What we did:

We developed the concept of the portal for CISM.

The World Skills Global Portal

The portal is the unified information platform easily adapting to different types of competitions. The cross-functional portal constructor can be used by the competitions’ management without the need to create a separate website for each event. The database of the sportsmen, teams and events will be shared in one platform. The new resource will gather the best representatives of the skilled occupations and the partners from different industrial areas.

What we did:

The portal is being currently developed.

The information and analytical system “The open budget for youth”

The modern information resource allowing the youth to receive the budget data of the ministries, the state institutions and the departments. The portal presents the information about the new industrial technologies and assists in finding a job locally or in a neighbouring region. The portal is a cross-functional tool to implement the Open State initiative for the youth.

What we did:

We developed the concept of the portal.

The information and analytical system “The open budget for children”

“The great adventure” internet portal tells children about the modern industrial achievements and breakthroughs. The kids learn in a game about the production of the machines and mechanisms, about the family and state budget planning principles. The portal is a cross-functional tool to implement the Open State initiative for children.

What we did:

We developed the concept of the portal implemented in 2016.

The Tatar Literature Centre

The Tatar literature centre global portal is a cultural project presenting a platform to the global audience interested in the Tatar literature. The recourse solves the high-level tasks of popularization and preservation of the Tatar culture and literature.

What we did:

We developed the concept of the portal implemented in 2016.
